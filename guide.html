What This Tool Is

Purpose

A public-safe demo of an Irish SME succession origination workflow.

Main job

Help a reviewer decide which businesses deserve research, a call, a watchlist note, or no further time.

Private vs public

The public site is sanitized. Analyst-only notes, credentials, and contact history should stay outside it.

What To Test First

Priority Research

Open the first 40 operating SME targets ranked ahead of ordinary sale listings.

Evidence Gate

Check whether a target is investment ready, origination ready, research needed, watchlist only, or discard.

Dual Engine Playground

Key Terms

Micro Engine

Scores company-level fit: founder/succession angle, business quality, deal readiness, and contactability.

Macro Engine

Scores outside context: sector demand, policy exposure, public spending, rates, labour, energy, and regulation.

Evidence Gate

The control layer that stops weak evidence from looking like an investment-ready opportunity.

Gate-adjusted score

The original score after evidence penalties. Weak sources lower the usable score.

Score penalty

The deduction applied when a company has weak or incomplete evidence.

Source confidence

How strong the public source trail is for the target.

Target universe type

Whether the row is an advertised sale listing, operating SME, public-profile target, or weak record.

Entity cluster

Rows that appear to describe the same business grouped together.

Source Labels

verified profile

A public company/profile source gives stronger legal identity or trading-status evidence.

live listing

A public broker or marketplace page shows transaction intent, but details still need checking.

public profile

A website, phone, email, or public profile suggests a real operating business.

needs verification

The row is useful only as a lead until legal identity or trading proof is improved.

Evidence Gate Statuses

investment_ready

Strong enough for serious IC-style treatment, subject to normal diligence.

origination_ready

Strong enough for research or a first-call decision.

research_needed

Interesting, but legal identity, source confidence, or trading proof still needs work.

watchlist_only

Keep an eye on it, but do not spend major time yet.

discard

Not suitable because evidence is too weak or the target is outside scope.

Review Decisions

advance

Move toward deeper diligence because the evidence and score both justify more work.

watch

Keep the company visible but wait for better evidence or a clearer succession angle.

research_more

Assign someone to fill the missing evidence before contact.

contact

Prepare a founder-friendly first call or confidential approach.

meeting

Track targets where a real discussion is now the next step.

decline

Remove from active attention because the evidence, fit, or target type is wrong.

Known Limitations

Not investment advice

The site is a decision-support demo, not an automatic investment approval tool.

Public-source limits

Some company-universe rows still need approved source metadata before investment-grade use.

No outreach automation

The tool suggests research and contact decisions but does not contact businesses.

No private CRM

Local review edits are browser-local unless exported; this is not yet a full CRM.
